About me

A small introduction.

Eva Xu

Student at Western University

I’m a curious thinker and strategic risk-taker with roots in marketing, business development, and product management. I have global experience working with larger corporates and ambitious startups, and am consistently drawn to creative ideas and innovative projects.

I’m inspired by wise words and strive to motivate others through my own, having spoken about human rights and cultural diffusion on a local and international scale. To date, I've shared the podium with past presidents Bill Clinton and George Bush, Olympian Meaghan Mikkelson, and survivors of genocides around the world.

I’m particularly fascinated by the applications of psychology in marketing fields, and am currently conducting research on mortality salience and its effect on consumer perception. I also have a huge passion for reading and writing -- on rainy days, you'll find me writing illustrative pieces and short fiction with a cup of hot tea in hand.

I'm always looking for learning opportunities, diverse experiences, and the next adventure. Shoot me a message if you want to connect!
